## 📈 Key Insights

- **Conversion Rate:** 11.27%  
- **Best Channel:** Cellular (14.2%)  
- **Peak Months:** May–July (~15%)  
- **Top Segment:** Management roles (16.8%)  
- **Trend:** Higher education → higher conversion  

---

## 🔍 Main Funnel Drivers

- **Contact Method:** Cellular outperforms telephone  
- **Seasonality:** Strong performance mid-year  
- **Job Type:** Management & admin convert best  
- **Education:** Graduates show higher conversion  
- **Funnel Drop-off:** High loss between contact and subscription  

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Focus on **cellular outreach**  
- Prioritize campaigns in **May–July**  
- Target **high-converting job segments**  
- Tailor messaging for **educated audiences**  
- Reduce low-performing **telephone campaigns**
